Valentino's Pizza Crust

Description

This is an excellent pizza crust recipe. I have found it is really good when baked on a pizza stone.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water(110 degrees F/45 degrees C
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2¼ teaspoons active dry years
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspon salt
  • 2½ cups all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Stir water, sugar and yeast together until dissolved.
  2. Add the olive oil and the salt.
  3. Stir in the fluor until well blended.
  4. Let dough rest for 10 minutes.
  5. Pat dough into pan or on to a pizza stone using fingers dipped in olive oil.
  6. If desired sprinkle basil, thyme or other seasonings on crust.
  7. Top with your favourite pizza toppings and bake for 15 to 20 minutes in a preheated 425 degree oven.
